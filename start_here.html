🌸 Welcome to SakuLink 🌸

What is SakuLink?

SakuLink is a helpful app for international travelers in Japan.
Just scan a QR code at a restaurant, and get a clear explanation of each dish in English.

🧭 It's like having a menu guide in your pocket—no Japanese required!

What You Can Do

  • 📷 Scan QR codes to read dish details in English
  • 🥢 Learn how to pronounce Japanese dish names
  • 📚 Explore articles on Japanese culture and food
  • ⚠️ Check main ingredients and possible allergens

What SakuLink Cannot Do

  • ❌ It doesn't include full menus for every restaurant
  • ❌ It can't place orders or make reservations
  • ❌ Some translations or photos may be simplified or symbolic

Important Notes

SakuLink provides general explanations of typical ingredients, cooking methods, and background for Japanese dishes as they are commonly prepared in Japan.
However, actual ingredients, preparation, and set menu combinations may vary depending on the restaurant.
We encourage you to enjoy tasting and discovering the unique flavors each place has to offer.
