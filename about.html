About SakuLink

SakuLink is a mobile-friendly web app designed to help international travelers in Japan easily understand Japanese dishes. By scanning a QR code provided by participating restaurants, users can view simple descriptions of meals in English and other languages.

Our goal is to make Japanese food culture more accessible and enjoyable for everyone—without language barriers.
